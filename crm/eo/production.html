Attendee Journey

The AccessFuel Platform digitally re-creates your event so you can understand who went where, when.
Think of it as holding the 'magic wand' to your event. Where you can answer any and every question about your audience.


Benefits include optimizing the attendee journey and experience to ensure they go

For example, did Marketing VP's attend all the sessions they should to learn and get the most from the event?
Did they visit all the exhibitor booths? Did they attend the sessions most important and valuable to them (including accreditation)?

Overall, does the make-up of your attendees (and their interests) aligne with the goals and objectives of your Organization... as well as the content?
